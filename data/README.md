# Data

- **S2 calibration:** `quest_ord.txt` (72 surveys × 8 count cells) is included in
  `../s2_calibration/`. Source: question-order survey counts via **OSF project n9q2m**
  (Kellen, Singmann, & Batchelder), a re-analysis of the data from Wang, Z., Solloway, T.,
  Shiffrin, R. M., & Busemeyer, J. R. (2014), *Context effects produced by question orders
  reveal quantum nature of human judgments*, PNAS 111(26). Public, attribution retained.
- **S1:** no human-subject data is included (study pending IRB approval).
  See `../s1_elicitation/PROTOCOL_google_form.md` for the pre-registered instrument.
